ZK Crowdfunding Auditor

Generate commitments, prove the total, and verify it end-to-end with a single click.

Donation Inputs

Enter four donor amounts. You can supply randomness values or let the backend generate them.

Proof Status

Fill in the donations and click Prove & Verify to see the zero-knowledge flow.

How It Works

  1. Commitments are produced with the Poseidon hash for each donor amount and its secret randomness.
  2. The Groth16 prover generates a zk-SNARK showing the commitments are valid and the public total matches.
  3. The verifier checks the proof using the exported verification key, confirming the audit without revealing private donations.

All heavy lifting runs locally through the pre-built circuit artifacts in this repository.
